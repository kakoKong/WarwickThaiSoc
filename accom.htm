Accommodation Recommend

Location

On Campus

On Campus accommodation is a good choice if you want to wake up at 8:30am for 9am lecture. The benefits you will get from staying on-campus is that you don't really have spare a time for transportation since you can just have a 5-min walk to your lecture hall.

Pros:
You can wake up late, easy to make friend with foreigners

Cons:
Room might be alittle expensive and not as nice as accommodation in the city

View On-campus Accommodation Recommend

Coventry

Large City with alot of asians restaurant. It is not far from University where it usually takes about 20-30min of bus ride. There are place for shoppings and alot of Boba shop. It might be alittle dangerous at night but if you are not walking alone at night then it should be fine.

Pros:
Karaoke, Hotpot, Korean BBQ, all in a 5 min walking range
Accommodation is nice and new.

Cons:
Can be alittle unsafe to walk alone at night

View Coventry's Accommodation Recommend

Leamington Spa

Leamington Spa is a beautiful city where you can be shopping and eating here, the city itself is quite clean and more organised. However, it does takes about 35-45min of bus ride to arrive at University. There are more housing and apartment than student accommodation.

Pros:
City is nice, feel safe walking around

Cons:
Less Accommodation Choice, Long Bus ride
