About Us

Warwick Thai Society is a vibrant community of Thai students in the University of Warwick, Coventry, United Kingdom. There are multiple activities and events organised by us every year. Like us to get the most updated events and keep in touch with your Thai friends. This official fan page is opened for everyone! Please feel free to come and join us! Sawasdee ^/\^
